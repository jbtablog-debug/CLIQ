History of B-Hive Cafe

B-Hive Café was established on February 14, 2023, in Bulala, Vigan City, Ilocos Sur. It was founded by Neil Bryan Valdez and Shirley Valdez, who also serve as the café’s managers and staff.

The name “B-Hive Café” was inspired by the place itself—where bees used to fly around and build their hives. Initially, the owners struggled to come up with a name, as every idea they thought of was repeatedly rejected. However, one day, they noticed a large number of bees gathering around the area, and it sparked an idea—to name the café “B-Hive,” symbolizing a home for bees, just as the café would become a warm and welcoming home for customers. This natural connection led to the unique and memorable identity of the café.
