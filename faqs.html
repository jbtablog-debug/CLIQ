FAQs using CLIQ

What is CLIQ?

CLIQ is a mobile ordering application developed specifically for B-Hive Café to make the ordering process faster, easier, and more convenient. It allows customers to browse the café’s menu, customize their drinks or food, place orders, and track their order status in real-time. The app also helps reduce manual errors and waiting times, giving both customers and staff a smoother café experience.

How do I create an account on CLIQ?

To create an account on CLIQ, simply visit our website and click on the "Sign Up" button. Fill in the required information such as your name, email address, and password. Once you submit the form, you'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

Is CLIQ free to use?

CLIQ offers both free and premium plans. The free plan includes basic features suitable for small teams, while the premium plans provide advanced functionalities such as enhanced security, larger storage capacity, and priority support. You can choose the plan that best fits your team's needs.

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, you can reset it by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you instructions on how to create a new password.

Is my data secure on CLIQ?

We take data security very seriously at CLIQ. We implement industry-standard encryption protocols to protect your data both in transit and at rest. Additionally, we regularly update our security measures to safeguard against potential threats.
